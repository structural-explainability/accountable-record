# Mappings

Mappings connect local, domain, institutional, or external vocabulary to AR
structure.

Authoritative source data lives in:

```text
data/mappings/
```

Subject-specific mapping source data lives in:

```text
data/subject-mappings/
```

## Purpose

AR does not require every adopter to abandon existing vocabulary.

Instead, a profile may map existing fields, terms, classifications, or subject
kinds into AR structural roles.

Mappings make those connections explicit, inspectable, and citable.

## Types of Mappings

AR may use mappings for:

- field roles;
- field mappings;
- subject kinds;
- subject vocabularies;
- profile-specific classifications;
- external standards.

## Boundary

Mappings preserve structural relationships.

They do not decide whether the mapped vocabulary is true, authoritative,
complete, or universally correct.
